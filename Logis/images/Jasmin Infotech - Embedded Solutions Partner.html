Innovative Embedded Solutions & Services

Pure Play Embedded - Core Logic - End to End Product Development - Assured Quality - Global Delivery. These are the core ideas around which Jasmin Infotech has been shaped to become a Reliable Embedded Solutions Partner for major OEM's and Tier 1's across the globe. With extensive expertise and deep domain knowledge we have built tested and production ready solutions in Automotive, SMART Audio & Connectivity and Embedded Testing.

JI's ethos is also enshrined in our R&D focused culture. State of the Art Infrastructure and Domain Specific labs and dedicated team support enables us to support the customers in developing and integrating modern technologies while ensuring time to market. To summarise - JI's focus is to Help You Innovate.
